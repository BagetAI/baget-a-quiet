The Antithesis of the Noise War.

In a city defined by its volume, we choose stillness. A Quiet is a community space built on a strict sub-85dB policy. We believe sound should be a ritual, not a background noise.

Our space is designed for the conversation that happens after the first drink, and the deep listening that happens before the needle lifts. No shouting. No crowds. Just the fidelity of the groove.

"Where the ritual of selection meets the craft of the cocktail."
02.

The Curated Archive.

We don't collect records; we curate essentials. Our shop focuses exclusively on high-fidelity pressings of Jazz, Soul, and Rare Groove. From Blue Note first-pressings to modern Japanese audiophile reissues.
